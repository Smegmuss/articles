Meditation Techniques for Streetlifters

Guided Meditation for Pre-Workout Hype

Don’t just jump into your workout cold. Try a short guided meditation before you lift:

  • Put on headphones and listen to a 5-minute hype meditation (search YouTube for “pre-workout guided meditation”)
  • Focus on your breath and imagine energy flowing into your muscles
  • Repeat a mantra: “I am strong. I’m a beast. I crush every rep.”

This primes your mind and body for peak performance.

Mindful Breathing to Crush Plateaus

Stuck on a plateau? Mindful breathing can break you free. Practice box breathing between sets:

  • Inhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds
  • Exhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds

This calms nerves, increases oxygen flow, and resets your focus. Next time you’re grinding out weighted pull ups, use mindful breathing to power through.

Visualization: See Yourself Becoming a Beast

Elite athletes swear by visualization. Before your next street lifting session:

  • Close your eyes and picture yourself nailing a new PR—weighted dips, ring muscle ups, whatever your goal
  • Feel the strength, see the movement, hear the crowd
  • Let that vision hype you up and fuel your workout

Visualization turns goals into reality. Your brain can’t tell the difference between imagination and real experience—use that to your advantage.
